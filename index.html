Yes. Callova uses the latest AI voice technology. The voice is natural, warm and professional. Most callers cannot distinguish it from a human receptionist.
Do I need to change my phone number? +
No. You keep your existing number. We configure call forwarding so Callova answers on your behalf. The process takes approximately five minutes.
What happens with questions it cannot answer? +
Callova handles the most common 80% of calls with confidence. For anything more complex, it takes the caller's details and sends you an immediate notification to follow up.

Your AI receptionist can be live within 24 hours of signing up. We manage the entire setup — you simply provide your business information.
Is there a long-term contract? +
No. Month-to-month only. Cancel at any time. We are confident in what Callova delivers and do not rely on locking clients in.
Which types of business does this suit? +
Any service business that relies on incoming calls — auto repair shops, dental clinics, law firms, beauty salons, physiotherapy practices, and more.
